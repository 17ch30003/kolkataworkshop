    About Us

    KSHITIJ

    Kshitij is the annual techno-management symposium of IIT Kharagpur. Since its inception in 2004, it has grown exponentially and has become the largest symposium of its kind in Asia. Kshitij comprises a diverse array of highly competitive technical and managerial events which attract the participation of the best brains of the leading engineering and management institutes from all across the country.

    KOLKATA WORKSHOP

    As part of our journey to drive young brilliant minds towards innovation, we organise a pre-fest workshop to give exposure to the students about autonomous robotics and also launching new enthralling events for its 17th edition of Kshitij. This year the Kolkata Workshop will be conducted at Vidya Mandir Auditorium Inside Birla High School, Kolkata wherein, Robotics enthusiasts will get an opportunity to probe deeper into the world of robotics in the most ingenious way.
